Dhamal
Actors Quotes
"Dhamaal" is a Bollywood comedy about four lazy friends—Roy, Manav, Adi, and Boman—who discover the secret to a hidden treasure from a dying man. Their hilarious road trip to Goa turns into a series of crazy events filled with twists, confusion, and non-stop laughter, especially with a determined police inspector on their trail.

Actors

  • Riteish Deshmukh as Roy
  • Arshad Warsi as Aditya Shrivastav (Adi)
  • Javed Jaffrey as Manav Shrivastav
  • Aashish Chaudhary as Boman
  • Sanjay Dutt as Inspector Kabir Nayak
